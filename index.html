Boardwalks

LIKE SIDEWALKS, ONLY AWESOME

If you're anything like me (and you are) you're sick to death of those gosh darned sidewalks. They're either coated in old chewing gum or teeming with children playing hopscotch badly. And don't get me started on cracks.

Well enough is enough. My friends, there is another way: the boardwalk. All the ease of a flat surface to walk on, only now with wooden planks and a colourful cast of people nearby. Don't believe me? Well then, have a gander at this 'Internet Video', why dontcha?

Okay. Admittedly, boardwalks themselves are not heavily featured in the video, but my argument is solid.
